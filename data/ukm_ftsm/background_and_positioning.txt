Faculty:
- Faculty of Information Science and Technology
- Fakulti Teknologi dan Sains Maklumat
- Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM)
- Bangi, Selangor, Malaysia

Background:
- According to the official FTSM background page, computing-related courses have been offered at UKM since the university was established in 1970.
- FTSM later developed into a full faculty focused on information science and technology.
- The faculty presents itself as a place for teaching, research, innovation, and industry engagement in computing and information technology fields.

Why choose FTSM:
- The official "Why Choose Us" page highlights rankings and positioning connected to computer science, information systems, data science, and engineering-related subject areas.
- The same page states that the faculty emphasizes competition-based learning through student special interest groups.
- The faculty also highlights professional certification alignment, accreditation by MQA, international mobility opportunities, flexible learning, and industry-linked readiness programmes.
- Undergraduate students are described as participating in academic courses, student special interest groups, final year projects, and about five months of industrial attachment.
- Postgraduate students are described as being associated with the faculty's research centers and research environment.

Research centers mentioned on official pages:
- CAIT: Center for Artificial Intelligence
- SOFTAM: Center for Software Technology and Management
- CYBER / Siber: Center for Cyber Security

Campus and location notes:
- The faculty is located at the UKM Bangi campus in Selangor.
- The faculty map page states that FTSM is located near the Faculty of Engineering and Built Environment.
- The official website also highlights the broader green campus environment around UKM.

Advisory note:
- Rankings, programme features, and positioning statements can change over time.
- Users should verify the latest details on the official FTSM website if they need current promotional or institutional information.
