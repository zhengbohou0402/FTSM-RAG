UKM FTSM Facilities and Services
Source URL:
- https://ftsm.ukm.my/v6/facility

General facility information:
- The official facilities page provides information about space reservation, rental, and service access.
- For space reservation, the page lists contact number 03 8921 6666 and email norzaima@ukm.edu.my.

ICT Services Counter:
- Location: Ground Floor, Block G
- Operating days: Monday to Friday
- Operating hours: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
- Services listed:
  - Help desk
  - Computer repair
  - Software installation

Computer labs and internet:
- Locations mentioned: Block C, Block G, and Block H
- Operating days: Monday to Friday
- Operating hours: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
- The page says the faculty provides computer labs for both undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- It also mentions open Wi-Fi hotspots for students using their own laptops.

Research laboratories:
- Locations mentioned: Block C, Block G, and Block H
- Operating days: Monday to Friday
- Operating hours: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
- The official page says the research laboratories support scientific research, especially for master's and PhD students.

Lecture halls:
- Locations mentioned: Block C and Block G
- Operating days: Monday to Friday
- Operating hours: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
- The page says lecture halls can support lectures for around 100 students and conferences for more than 200 participants in a single seating.

Lecture rooms:
- Locations mentioned: Block B and Block G
- Operating days: Monday to Friday
- Operating hours: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
- The page says classrooms can generally fit around 60 to 70 students.
- It also says classrooms are equipped with audio-visual, media, and computing support.
