Industrial training:
- The official industrial training page says the Industrial Training Programme is compulsory for students at the Faculty of Information Science and Technology.
- The programme is intended to expose students to a real working environment and practical experience.
- The page says the programme helps students enhance skills and develop competencies needed to become professionals, entrepreneurs, or researchers in information technology-related fields.

Industrial training ecosystem:
- The page references the FTSM Industrial Training System (FITS).
- It also references Karnival Latihan Industri dan Kerjaya (KILK), an event where students present skills gained during internships.
- Companies may participate in KILK by giving industrial talks, opening booths, or joining related activities.
- The industrial training page lists hejimftsm@ukm.edu.my as a contact for more information about KILK-related participation.
