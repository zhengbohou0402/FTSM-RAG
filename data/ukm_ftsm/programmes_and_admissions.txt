Undergraduate programmes:
- The official undergraduate page says FTSM offers bachelor degree programmes in:
  - Bachelor in Computer Science with Honours
  - Bachelor in Information Technology with Honours
  - Bachelor in Software Engineering with Honours
- The accreditation page also lists:
  - Bachelor of Software Engineering (Multimedia System Development) with Honours
  - Bachelor of Software Engineering (Information System Development) with Honours

Undergraduate admissions notes from official pages:
- Applications for full-time study programmes for STPM, matriculation, foundation, diploma, or equivalent routes are handled through the Ministry of Higher Education Malaysia online application process.
- The undergraduate page says advertisements for those programmes are typically published between December and February each year.
- The page also says second-channel intake advertisements for full-time programmes are published in November each year and applications are submitted via UKM Online Application.
- The undergraduate page states that non-citizen and international candidates interested in full-time undergraduate study should apply through UKM's online application process.
- The same page states that, at the time of the published material, undergraduate courses at UKM use Bahasa Melayu as the medium of instruction.

Postgraduate programmes:
- The official master programme page says FTSM offers eleven master's programmes:
  - Master of Software Engineering
  - Master of Artificial Intelligence
  - Master of Information Technology
  - Master of Information Science
  - Master of Creative Media Technology
  - Master of Information System
  - Master of Computer Science
  - Master of Computing
  - Master of Cyber Security
  - Master of Health Informatics
  - Master of Data Science

Postgraduate structure notes:
- The master programme page indicates that some programmes are offered by coursework, some by coursework and research, some by module, and some in research-oriented formats.
- The page also provides postgraduate prospectus links and administrative forms.

Accreditation note:
- The accreditation page lists undergraduate and master's programme accreditation categories and should be used as the reference point for official programme status.

Admissions and FAQ notes from the Calon FTSM page:
- The page includes contact information for intake-related queries through intakeftsm@ukm.edu.my.
- It includes a FAQ that says administrative office hours are weekdays from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
- It notes that postgraduate application status can be checked through the School of Graduate Studies.
- It also says FTSM offers undergraduate, graduate, and Doctor of Philosophy studies.

Time-sensitive note:
- The Calon FTSM page contained a 2025 undergraduate screening notice with a submission period from 21 April 2025 to 25 June 2025.
- That intake window is date-specific and should not be treated as a standing schedule for future intakes.
- Users should always verify the latest admission cycle directly on the official FTSM or UKM admission page.
