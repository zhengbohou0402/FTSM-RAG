[Topic 1] UKM Registration Guide / UKM注册指南

Section: Online Registration / 线上注册
- Upload all currently available materials to the JoinUKM system.
- Suggested documents include tuition payment receipt, Eval, EMGS, and other documents already in hand.

Section: Health Check Process / 体检流程

Option A: UKM Health Centre / 校医院
- Print and fill in the "Report of Health Examination Form" in advance.
- Go to the UKM health centre.
- Go to counter/window 19.
- Submit the form and pay the medical examination fee of 250 MYR.
- Complete the checks in A3, A2, and A1.
- Return to A3 and wait for the report.
- Return to window 19 to submit the report.
- Wait for your number to be called and ask the doctor to stamp and sign.
- Go to the photocopy room on level 2 of the health centre to make copies. Approximate fee: 5 MYR, cash only.
- Return to window 19 and submit the original and photocopy.
- Scan the medical report and upload it to JoinUKM. Upload the original report together with the first page of the photocopy set.
- Important reminder: blood test and urine test are required. Drink more water in advance.

Option B: External Clinic / 校外医院
- Go to an approved external clinic or designated hospital for the medical examination.
- After receiving the medical report, bring the X-ray film and the report to the UKM health centre.
- Queue for the doctor to stamp and sign the report.
- Make photocopies on level 2 of the health centre. Approximate fee: 5 MYR, cash only.
- Scan the medical report and upload it to JoinUKM. Upload the original report together with the first page of the photocopy set.

Section: Verification and Visa Renewal / 认证与换签
- After the medical examination is completed, go to UKMShape.
- Verify whether all JoinUKM documents have been uploaded completely.
- Verify tuition payment, personal bond / deposit, and visa fee payment.
- Verify academic credentials. Bring original and photocopies of graduation certificate, degree certificate, and notarized documents.
- For visa renewal / endorsement, first scan the QR code and fill in the form, then submit:
  - medical report
  - Eval
  - eVisa
  - passport

Section: Passport Collection / 领取护照
- On the second day after EMGS reaches 100%, bring the temporary passport receipt/document to PKP for collection.
- Collection location: Pusanika, level 4.

Special Reminders / 特别提醒
- Bring as many documents as possible during in-person registration, including originals and photocopies.
- Bring a laptop in case temporary uploads or form submissions are needed.
- On-site payment supports cash, Visa, and Mastercard. UnionPay is not supported.

[Topic 2] UKM Renewal Guide / UKM续签指南

Required Documents / 所需资料
- attendance report / 出勤报告
- academic results / 成绩报告
- scanned passport copy including cover / 护照扫描件（含封面）
- renewal application form / 续签申请表
- screenshot from student financial system / 学生财务系统截图
- student confirmation letter / 学生确认信
- visa processing fee / 签证处理费
- EMGS fee / EMGS费用

Renewal Steps / 续签步骤
- Fill in the renewal Google Form and wait for the email notification.
- When EMGS reaches 50%, go to PKP and submit the passport.
- On the second day after EMGS reaches 100%, bring the temporary passport receipt/document to PKP to collect the passport.

Renewal Form and Google Form / 续签申请表和续签谷歌表格
- Official page: https://www.ukm.my/ukmshape/renewal--application/

[Topic 3] UKM Grab Destination Guide / UKM Grab指南

Recommended destination names when using Grab:
- Library / Haomiba / Visa office (PKP): Pusanika - UKM
- Faculty: FTSM (Fakulti Teknologi & Sains Maklumat - UKM) or Jumpa Cafe FTSM
- Health centre: Pusat Kesihatan
- UKMShape: UKMShape UKM Bangi

[Topic 4] UKM Core Systems / UKM各个系统

System 1: SMPWEB Student Profile / SMPWEB 学生档案
- URL: https://smplucee.ukm.my/smpweb
- Main use: semester course registration

System 2: UKM FOLIO Learning System / UKM FOLIO 上课系统
- URL: https://ukmfolio.ukm.my/
- Main use: course instructors, assignments, teaching materials, and course information

System 3: UKM SISWAMAIL Student Email / UKM SISWAMAIL 学生邮箱
- First-time setup:
  - Log in to https://appsmu.ukm.my/speep/index.php
  - Initial password is the passport number
  - Complete the mailbox application
- Subsequent login:
  - URL: http://mail.google.com/a/siswa.ukm.edu.my

System 4: UKM E-KEWANGAN Financial System / UKM E-KEWANGAN 财务系统
- URL: https://ekewangan.ukm.my/signin
- Main use: student billing and financial status

System 5: UKM Research and Publication Search / UKM 查教授发表文章和研究方向
- URL: https://ukmsarjana.ukm.my/
- Main use: search professors' publications and research areas

System 6: UKM Library / UKM Library
- URL: https://eresourcesptsl.ukm.remotexs.co/
- Main use: library electronic resources access
